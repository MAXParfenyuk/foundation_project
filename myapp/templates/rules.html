Welcome to the Blackjack Game!

Here you can play the classic game of Blackjack against the computer. Try your luck and see if you can beat the dealer.

Game Rules:

  1. Each player is dealt two cards.
  2. Players can choose to "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep their current hand).
  3. The goal is to get as close to 21 points as possible without going over.
  4. Cards 2 through 10 are worth their face value. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points. Aces can be worth either 1 or 11 points.
  5. If a player's hand exceeds 21 points, they lose the game.
  6. If both the player and the dealer have the same hand value, the game is a push and the player's bet is returned.
  7. If a player wins, they receive their bet back plus an amount equal to their bet. If the player has a Blackjack (an Ace and a 10-point card), they win 1.5 times their bet.
